Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Reserve Smoke ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the Reserve Smoke Inventory Manager App ("App").

1. Information We Collect

This App is a private internal business tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. All data entered into the App (product names, inventory counts, order quantities) is stored locally on your device using browser local storage.

2. Clover Integration

If you choose to connect the App to your Clover POS account, your Clover API credentials (Merchant ID and API Token) are stored locally in your browser only. These credentials are used solely to communicate directly with Clover's API on your behalf. We do not store, log, or transmit your credentials to any third party.

3. Data Storage

All inventory data is stored in your browser's local storage on your device. No data is sent to or stored on any external server operated by Reserve Smoke.

4. Third Party Services

The App may communicate with Clover's API (api.clover.com) using credentials you provide. Please refer to Clover's Privacy Policy at clover.com for information on how Clover handles your data.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend using password protection on any device running this App.
